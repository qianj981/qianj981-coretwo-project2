Bumpy
Bumpy surfaces poke beneath the skin like the ridges on a cobblestone path. Tiny raised bumps jab and wiggle unpredictably, making you slow down, step carefully, or run your fingers over them out of curiosity.
Cold
Cold sneaks in when your fingers dip into an ice-cold stream. A chill sharpens attention, stretches time, and makes every stone and leaf feel deliberate under your touch.
Sticky
Stickiness grabs your fingers like melted honey on a spoon. Skin resists being pulled away as moisture or resin clings, reminding you of the fuzzy line between yourself and whatever wants to hold on.
Plush
Plush surfaces give way like sinking your hands into a heap of soft fleece or a fluffy stuffed animal. Dense fibers cradle your fingers, spreading pressure evenly and inviting you to linger longer, luxuriating in the softness.
Warm
Warmth spreads when you bury your hand in a cat or dog’s fur. It feels alive, cozy, and undeniably close, a comforting touch you remember and never quite forget.
